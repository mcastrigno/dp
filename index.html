The Building

A Landmark on West Roseberry Road

Donnelly Place is a beautifully designed mixed-use building in the heart of Donnelly, Idaho. With its distinctive red roof and mountain-lodge architecture, it's one of the most recognizable addresses in Valley County.

Mountain-Lodge Architecture

Distinctive red metal roof, warm wood accents, and classic craftsman detailing.

Modern Interiors

Clean, well-finished spaces with quality carpeting, lighting, and trim throughout.

Secure Key-Code Access

Resident and tenant peace of mind, 24/7.

Central Location

150 W Roseberry Rd — walkable, visible, and central to Donnelly.
